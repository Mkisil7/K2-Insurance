Neighbors Helping Neighbors Protect What Matters.

Our Story

Founded by Jonah Katz and Ethan Kisilevich. We formed K2 to give Florida property owners a clear, modern path to coverage that fits.

We operate as an independent brokerage with access to multiple carriers. That lets us tailor coverage, align deductibles, and keep pricing competitive without locking you to one option.

We keep it simple: explain risk, compare options, and support you at renewal and at claim time.
